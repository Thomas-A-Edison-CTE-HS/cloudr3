Hover over this section to reveal more details about the game, that way you can follow the instructions carefully to play each level!

In this game of, "Spot the Difference" there are 3 levels you can choose from depending on how difficult you'd like to play the game.

These 3 levels are:

  1. Easy
  2. Medium
  3. Hard

Throughout each level, you are able to see multiple objects, you have only a few seconds to look at each of them. After that, BOOM! Your screen turns black and turns back on shortly after.

Only up to 3 out of all the objects have changed. As you progress through each level, spotting these differences will only get harder and harder. Can you spot the difference?
